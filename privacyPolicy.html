Privacy Policy for familytrip app(צובחוץ)

Use of Camera, Location, and In-App Purchases

Introduction

This app is designed to host riddle games and provide a fun and engaging experience for families. In order to do so, the app requires access to certain features on your device, including the camera and location services while using the app. the need for camera and file permission is only for admins. We understand that privacy is important to our users, and we are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with this privacy policy.

Data Collection and Use

Our app requires access to your device's camera and location services in order to function properly and for the purpose of using the image taken there is a need to access files as well. The app does not share any data collected with third parties or store it on our servers. We only collect data to improve the user experience within the app. It’s important to mention that the need for access to use the camera and access files is only for admins and will grant to tour guides only therefore is not open to children of any age.

In-App Purchases

Our app offers in-app purchases. All purchases are processed through the Google Play store, and we do not collect or store any payment information. Google Play's own privacy policy applies to any payment information collected by Google Play.

Third-Party Services

Our app may include links to third-party services, such as social media platforms. Please note that these third-party services have their own privacy policies and we are not responsible for their privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for families and may be used by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that we may have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, please contact us immediately at itamararbel@gmail.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
